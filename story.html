Little Red Riding Hood Story  Demo

Below is our demo for you to test and understand the process. You will firstly scan the QR code on cover to get our app and then wile you are reading the story you will encounter with another QR code that placed in specific part of the story. You will scan that QR code with our app and you will be able to play the game that designed for this part of the story. Enjoy...
